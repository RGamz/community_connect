About Community Connect

Our Mission

Community Connect aims to bridge the gap between homeless individuals and their communities by highlighting their skills, stories, and potential.

Many homeless individuals have valuable skills and talents that could benefit local businesses and community projects. Our platform makes these connections possible.

By sharing information about homeless individuals in your area, you help create opportunities for employment, housing, and social support.

How It Works

1. Locate on Map

Find a homeless individual in your community who needs support or has skills to offer.

2. Add Their Profile

Submit their information including skills, needs, and location to our secure database.

3. Connect & Help

Community members can view profiles and offer assistance, employment, or resources.
